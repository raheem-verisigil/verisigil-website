Section 03

What Runtime Guard Does Today — Live in Production

1
Agent calls POST /v1/guard/verify before any action executes
2
Identity verified cryptographically — Ed25519 passport, shadow detection, expiry check
3
Trust score evaluated — live score 0.0–1.0, below 0.60 = automatic DENY
4
Policy applied — payment threshold, PII rules, blocked tools, action-specific rules
5
Decision returned in under 50ms — ALLOW · DENY · REQUIRE_HUMAN_APPROVAL
6
Approval request created automatically — unique URL, 24hr expiry, email sent to approver
7
Approver reviews and decides — standalone Approval Console, approve or reject with name and email
8
Decision cryptographically logged — immutable audit trail, Ed25519 signed, forever
