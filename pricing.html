Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Runtime Guard verification?
Every time your AI agent calls POST /v1/guard/verify before executing an action — that counts as one verification. The endpoint checks identity, trust score, shadow detection and policy in under 50ms and returns ALLOW, DENY or REQUIRE_HUMAN_APPROVAL.
Can I try VeriSigil for free?
Yes. Use our free scanner at verisigilai.com/scanner.html and issue your first agent passport at verisigilai.com/quickstart.html — no credit card required. Integration partners get 30 days free on any plan.
Does VeriSigil help with EU AI Act compliance?
Yes. Every plan includes cryptographic audit trails, human oversight enforcement via Article 14, and compliance reports. VeriSigil was built specifically for EU AI Act alignment — the August 2026 deadline is 84 days away.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major cards, bank transfers, and mobile money via Paystack. For enterprise customers we also accept wire transfer and can issue invoices in USD, EUR or GBP. Contact raheem@verisigilai.com for invoice-based billing.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your Paystack account. Your agents and audit trails remain accessible for 90 days after cancellation.
What is Shadow Detection™?
Shadow Detection monitors your registered AI agents in real time for clone and impersonation attempts. If another agent tries to use your agent's identity, VeriSigil detects and blocks it instantly — and logs the event to your immutable audit trail.
